WHAT IS RARE DOGE?
Rare Doge is the first of its kind BEP20 token on the binance smart chain.

Rare Doge has a very low supply. On top of Rare Doge being a low supply token, it also has a staking model that pays a high APY but also reduces the supply of the token even more.
TOKENOMICS
LOW SUPPLY
The initial supply of RD tokens is 3000 tokens. Over time, based on the staking and supply control mechanism the supply will get lower and lower.
STAKING
When an individual stakes their RD tokens the initial deposit amount is burned. The tokens gets reminted by the smart contract at a default daily percentage of 0.10% per day for each individual stake. However, individuals can use bnb to increase a specific stakes daily percent pay out from a default rate of 0.10% to a maximum rate of 2%. The APY for each stake is 50%.
SUPPLY BURN MECHANISM
When the price of the token is lower than the previous day price the smart contract will be triggered to burn 1% of the entire token supply.

RARE DOGE IS AUDITED
All smart contracts created by the Rare Doge team will be audited and reviewed by a third party company. This is to ensure that the smart contracts work and to ensure industry level of security.
Haze Crypto
Haze Security Group audits smart contracts, while reducing costs for companies producing these contracts, also examines customer security and contract risk.
